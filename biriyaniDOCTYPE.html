Our Story

The Art of
Dum Cooking

Biriyani Adda was born from a single belief — exceptional biryani should be for everyone. Nestled in the heart of Vijayanagar, Bengaluru, we brought the soul of Hyderabadi dum biryani straight to your neighbourhood.

Every handi is sealed with dough and slow-cooked over a patient flame, locking in the essence of whole spices, saffron, and tender meat until each grain of rice is perfectly fragrant and fluffy.
